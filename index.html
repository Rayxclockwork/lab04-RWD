About Me

I'm Raven, a software developer from Kansas City, Missouri. I attend Code Fellows in Seattle, Washington.

My history with software development is pretty short. I was introduced to it, mainly as a career option, by my parents' best friend. A suggestion to get me out of dead-end jobs, I did some research, began teaching myself, got super motivated, and decided to go to school for it.
I have a small, orange cat, Sinead, 2 sisters, and a deep love of stories with strong female characters - Buffy the Vampire Slayer, Game of Thrones, Ella Enchanted (the book. The movie is a disaster.)
